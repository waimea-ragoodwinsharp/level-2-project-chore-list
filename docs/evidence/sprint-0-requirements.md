# Project Requirements

## Identified Problem or Need

In my house, we have no organizatin of our chores to remind who needs to do what. My website will be a lest to show what chores are needing to be done. 


## End-User Requirements

My mother needs it to be easily readable, look good, and be able to be edited. 


## Proposed Solution

I intend to have my website have a functional list of chores and reset at the end of each day so chores don't need to be added over and over again.
